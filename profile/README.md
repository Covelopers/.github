## What We Do

Covelopers operates on two fronts.

<br/>

### Covelopers Academy

For aspiring and junior developers who are ready to do real work — not another tutorial, not another certificate. We put you inside an actual engineering environment:

- Real project workflows: Jira, documentation, sprints, standups
- Live projects worked on alongside experienced engineers
- A CV built through genuine, demonstrable output — not classroom exercises

The Academy exists because no certificate has ever proven work ethic. We prove it through what you ship.

<br/>

### Covelopers

Our commercial engineering arm — skilled engineers building products that matter:

- Client work across the UK: websites, systems, and custom tools for businesses and organisations
- Internal commercial products designed, built, and maintained by the team
- Events, community hackathons, and industry partnerships

## Our Philosophy

> *"In a lot of industries you don't need a degree, you need the mental acumen and work ethic. You can learn anything on the fly in most jobs, you cannot simply change your work ethic."*

The system — CVs, GPAs, degrees — has no reliable mechanism to build or measure drive, consistency, or diligence.

**Covelopers does.**
